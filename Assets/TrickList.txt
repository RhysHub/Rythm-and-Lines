Ollie Flip Tricks
Ollie – RS Up
Kickflip – RS Up + Right
Heelflip – RS Up + Left
FS Pop-Shuvit – RS Left
BS Pop-Shuvit – RS Right
Varial Kickflip – RS flick Down/Left then Up/Right
Varial Heelflip – RS flick Down/Right then Up/Left
360 Flip – RS Left → Down → Up/Right
Laserflip – RS Right → Down → Up/Left
Hardflip – RS Down → Down/Right → Up
Inward Heelflip – RS Down → Down/Left → Up
FS 360 Pop-Shuvit – RS Down/Right then drag Left
BS 360 Pop-Shuvit – RS Down/Left then drag Right
360 Hardflip – RS Right → Down → Up
360 Inward Heelflip – RS Left → Down → Up
Nollie Flip Tricks
The same principles apply, but popped from the nose instead of the tail.

Nollie – RS Up then Down
Nollie Kickflip – RS Up + Down/Right
Nollie Heelflip – RS Up + Down/Left
Nollie FS Pop-Shuvit – RS Up + drag Right
Nollie BS Pop-Shuvit – RS Up + drag Left
Nollie Varial Kickflip – RS Up/Left + Down/Right
Nollie Varial Heelflip – RS Up/Right + Down/Left
Nollie 360 Flip – RS Up/Left → Up → Down/Right
Nollie Laserflip – RS Up/Right → Up → Down/Left
Nollie Hardflip – RS Up → slight Right → Down/Right
Nollie Inward Heelflip – RS Up → slight Left → Down/Left
Nollie FS 360 Pop-Shuvit – RS Up/Left → drag Up/Right
Nollie BS 360 Pop-Shuvit – RS Up/Right → drag Up/Left
Nollie 360 Hardflip – RS Right → Up → Down/Right
Nollie 360 Inward Heelflip – RS Left → Up → Down/Left
Flip Trick Modifiers
Late Flip – RS Down + Up/Right mid-air
Held Flip (Doubleflip) – RS Down + Up/Right, hold in-air
Boned Ollie – Hold RS Up
Front Foot One-Foot Ollie – Press A/X while airborne
Back Foot One-Foot Ollie – Press X/Square while airborne

Grind Tricks
To grind, approach a rail or ledge at an angle, pop an Ollie, and input the right stick direction for the trick. Stance decides whether it’s Frontside or Backside.

50-50 Grind (FS/BS) – RS Down + Up
5-0 Grind (FS/BS) – Hold RS Down in-air
Nose Grind (FS/BS) – Hold RS Up in-air
Crooked (FS/BS) – RS Up/Left in-air
Overcrook (FS/BS) – RS Up/Right in-air
Salad (FS/BS) – RS Down/Right in-air
Smith (FS/BS) – RS Right + Up in-air
Feeble (FS) – RS Left + Up in-air
Willy (FS/BS) – RS Left + Down in-air
Over Willy (FS/BS) – RS Right + Down in-air
Boardslide (FS/BS) – RS Left in-air
Lipslide (FS/BS) – RS Right in-air
Noseslide (FS/BS) – RS Up + Left in-air
Tailslide (FS/BS) – RS Down + Left in-air
Bluntslide (FS/BS) – RS Down + Right in-air
Nose Blunt (FS/BS) – RS Up + Left in-air
Exit Grind/Slide – LS Left or Right
Grab Tricks
Grabs are the flashiest way to style your air time. They’re split into Basic, Modified, One-Foot, No-Foot, and Ground-based grabs.

Basic Grabs
BS Grab – Hold RT/R2
FS Grab – Hold LT/L2
Double Grab – Hold LT/L2 + RT/R2
Tail Grab – RS Up + LT/L2
Nose Grab – RS Down + RT/R2
Stale – RS Right + LT/L2
Weddle – RS Right + RT/R2
Crail – RS Down + LT/L2
Seatbelt – RS Up + RT/R2
Rocket Air – RS Down + RT/R2 + LT/L2
Modified Grabs
Nosebone – LT/L2 + RS Up
Tailbone – LT/L2 + RS Down
Tuck Knee – LT/L2 + RS Right
Stiffy – LT/L2 + RS Left
BS Tweak – RT/R2 + RS Down
Melon – RT/R2 + RS Up
Crossbone – RT/R2 + RS Left
Method – RT/R2 + RS Right
…plus advanced variations like Japan Air, Shifty Seatbelt, and Weddle Nosebone.
One-Foot Grabs
Frigid Air – LT/L2 + A/X
FS One-Foot – LT/L2 + X/Square
Judo Air – RT/R2 + X/Square
One-Foot Air – RT/R2 + A/X
Benihana – RS Up → RT/R2 + X/Square
No-Foot Grabs
Airwalk – RS Down → RT/R2 + B/Circle
Tailwalk – RS Up → RT/R2 + B/Circle
Christ Air – RT/R2 + B/Circle
Superdude – LT/L2 + RT/R2 + B/Circle
Ground-Based Grabs
Frontside Ground Grab – LT/L2 while rolling
Backside Ground Grab – RT/R2 while rolling
Double Ground Grab – LT/L2 + RT/R2 while rolling
Coffin – LT/L2 + RT/R2 + A/X + X/Square
Handplants & Footplants
Handplants (FS/BS) – LB/L1 or RB/R1 at a lip
Bean Plant – RT/R2 + A/X
Fastplant (FS/BS) – RT/R2 or LT/L2 + X/Square
Boneless – LT/L2 + A/X
